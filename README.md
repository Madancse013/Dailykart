## 🧠 Overview

Dailykart is a mobile-first hyperlocal delivery platform that connects users with nearby grocery vendors, enabling fast and reliable delivery of fresh produce and essential items.

The platform is designed to:

* eliminate time spent on grocery shopping
* ensure freshness and quality
* provide a smooth, stress-free ordering experience

Currently focused on **Hoskote and nearby areas**, Dailykart aims to digitize local grocery ecosystems.

---

## 🔥 Problem

In many local areas:

* grocery shopping is time-consuming
* availability of fresh products is inconsistent
* local vendors lack digital presence
* delivery options are limited or unreliable

---

## 💡 Solution

Dailykart solves this by:

* connecting users with nearby vendors
* enabling quick mobile ordering
* ensuring fast delivery within local areas
* maintaining product freshness and trust

## 🏗️ Architecture (High-Level)

User → Mobile App → Backend APIs → Vendor System → Delivery Flow

* App handles UI and ordering
* Backend processes orders and routing
* Vendors fulfill requests
* Delivery completes order cycle
